Hi, I am Xian-Hao (Harry) Liao!
My stage name is Reno Gordon.


Harry Liao is an audiovisual designer with over three years of experience in various areas of design, including visual design, web development, photography, interactive art, promotional material design, and 3D modeling for prototyping.

Currently, Harry works as a DMI Archivist Assistant at MassArt, where he supports the institution's digital media archive. In his previous role as a 3D artist for Future House Music, He designed and adjusted 3D scenes using C4D, collaborated with clients to finalize artwork, and successfully released his work on popular platforms such as Spotify and YouTube. Harry also served as a graphic designer for ABC Design.tw, where the company represented ABC Design.de's products in Taiwan, he rolled out new product ideas, and facilitated cross-departmental work.

Harry holds a Bachelor’s degree in Design from the National Taichung University of Science and Technology, and he is currently pursuing a Master of Fine Arts in Dynamic Media Institute at Massachusetts College of Art and Design.
