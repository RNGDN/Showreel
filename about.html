Hi, I am Xian-Hao (Harry) Liao!
My stage name is Reno Gordon.


Harry Liao is a UX Designer and audiovisual creator with a passion for narrative and experience creation. He excels at bridging creative strategy with forward-looking technology, possessing extensive experience in UX design, visual design, 3D modeling, and web development.

Recently, Harry worked as a Web3 Event UX Designer at Gate.com, successfully delivering the RedBull F1 Team NFT collaboration page. He also served as an Adjunct Professor at Boston University, integrating creative strategies with software applications. Previously, he designed 3D animations for Future House Music, optimized customer experiences at The UPS Store, and facilitated visual design for ABC Design.tw.

Harry holds a Master of Fine Arts from the Dynamic Media Institute at the Massachusetts College of Art and Design, completed specialized training at the MIT Media Lab, and earned his Bachelor’s degree in Design from the National Taichung University of Science and Technology.
